## What is FitFileViewer?

FitFileViewer is a free, open-source desktop application for viewing and analyzing `.fit` files from Garmin and other fitness devices. It features:

- 🗺️ **Interactive Maps** - Visualize your GPS routes
- 📊 **Performance Charts** - Analyze speed, heart rate, elevation, and more
- 📋 **Data Tables** - Explore detailed activity records
- 💻 **Cross-Platform** - Works on Windows, macOS, and Linux
